Steps to clone files to your computer from Remote Repo.

1.Create a folder in a place of your choice like (Desktop).

2.Open gitBash

3.Navigate to the location of that folder through gitbash.

4.Open your github Remote Repo

5.Go to the Remote Repo of your choice that you want to copy files from

6.Click on the green button (Code).

7.Click on the copy HTTPS link.

8.Go back to your gitbash window

9.Type (git clone) and paste the copied URL after (git clone) and hit ENTER.

10.Then it will start cloning all the files from that specific Remote Repo into the folder of your choice.
